<p><p>The 3-piece ball valves market is categorized into manual, pneumatic, and electric types. Manual valves rely on a hand-operated lever for control, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for low-traffic applications. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for operation, enabling rapid switching and automation in industrial settings. Electric valves employ electric actuators for precise control, making them ideal for remote monitoring and automation processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs, with varying levels of complexity and automation suited to specific industries.</p></p>
