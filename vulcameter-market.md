<p><ul><li>Vulcanizer with Rotor</li><li>Rotorless Vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vulcameter market is divided into two main types: Vulcanizers with rotors and rotorless vulcanizers. Vulcanizers with rotors feature a rotating mechanism that ensures uniform mixing and heating of rubber compounds, enhancing efficiency in the vulcanization process. In contrast, rotorless vulcanizers utilize fixed molds and rely on external heating sources, providing greater flexibility for varying product designs. Both types cater to the rubber and tire industries, addressing different manufacturing needs and optimizing production capabilities.</p></p>
