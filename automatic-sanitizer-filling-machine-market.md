<p><p>The Automatic Sanitizer Filling Machine market comprises two primary types: liquid filling machines and gel filling machines. Liquid filling machines are designed to efficiently dispense varying viscosities of sanitizer liquids into containers, ensuring precise volume control. In contrast, gel filling machines focus on handling thicker, gel-based sanitizers, utilizing specialized mechanisms to prevent clogs and ensure smooth operation. Both types cater to the growing demand for sanitizing products, driven by health and safety concerns, particularly in the post-pandemic environment.</p></p>
