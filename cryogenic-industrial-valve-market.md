<p><p>The cryogenic industrial valve market includes various types designed for low-temperature applications. Ball valves offer reliable shut-off and flow control, while butterfly valves are compact and efficient for large volumes. Gate valves provide a straight flow path with minimal pressure drop, and globe valves facilitate precise flow regulation. Plug valves allow for quick shut-off, whereas check valves prevent backflow. Diaphragm valves offer clean, leak-free performance, and safety valves ensure system protection by releasing excess pressure, crucial for cryogenic processes.</p></p>
