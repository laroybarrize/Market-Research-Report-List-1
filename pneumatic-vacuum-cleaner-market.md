<p><p>The pneumatic vacuum cleaner market can be divided into two main types: explosion-proof and not explosion-proof. Explosion-proof vacuum cleaners are designed for environments with flammable materials, featuring specialized components that prevent ignition. These are crucial in industries like petroleum, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, not explosion-proof vacuum cleaners are suitable for general use in less hazardous environments where there is no risk of explosion. Their designs focus more on efficiency and versatility for everyday cleaning tasks.</p></p>
