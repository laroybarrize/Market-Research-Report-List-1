<p><p>The phosphoric ester market comprises several types based on their composition and application. Alcohol-based phosphoric esters are derived from alcohols and are widely used as surfactants and corrosion inhibitors in various industries. Ethoxylated alcohol-based esters combine ethylene oxide with alcohols, enhancing solubility and making them effective in detergents and emulsifiers. Ethoxylated phenol-based phosphoric esters, derived from phenols, offer strong performance in industrial applications, including plastics and coatings, due to their stability and efficiency as additives.</p></p>
