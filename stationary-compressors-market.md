<p><ul><li>Positive-Displacement Compressors</li><li>Centrifuga Compressors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary compressors market comprises various types, including positive-displacement compressors and centrifugal compressors. Positive-displacement compressors operate by trapping a specific volume of air and compressing it, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, centrifugal compressors utilize rotating impellers to increase air velocity and convert it into pressure, ideal for large-scale operations requiring consistent airflow. Other market segments may include rotary screw compressors, scroll compressors, and diaphragm compressors, each tailored for specific industrial needs and efficiency levels.</p></p>
