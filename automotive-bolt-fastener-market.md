<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive bolt (fastener) market is categorized into threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, like bolts and screws, feature spiraled designs that allow for secure connections in various engine and chassis components. They are essential for adjustable assemblies and provide torque control. In contrast, non-threaded fasteners, such as rivets and pins, create permanent or semi-permanent joins, offering robustness and stability in structural applications. Both types are critical for vehicle assembly and maintenance, ensuring safety and performance.</p></p>
