<p><p>Modular construction is an innovative building approach where structures are assembled from prefabricated modules manufactured in a factory setting before being transported to the construction site for final assembly. This method enhances efficiency, reduces waste, and accelerates project timelines. The modular construction market has been gaining traction due to its ability to deliver high-quality structures at a lower cost and with reduced environmental impact.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates a robust upward trajectory for the modular construction market, driven by increasing urbanization, a rising population, and a demand for affordable housing solutions.
