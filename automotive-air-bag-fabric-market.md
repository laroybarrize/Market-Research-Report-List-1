<p><p>The automotive airbag fabric market comprises various types, primarily categorized into plain weave, twill weave, and others. Plain weave fabrics offer strength and durability due to their simple interlacing pattern, making them ideal for basic airbags. Twill weave, characterized by its diagonal pattern, provides better tensile strength and flexibility, enhancing airbag performance. Other fabric types may include specialty weaves designed for specific applications, such as enhanced abrasion resistance or lighter weight, catering to diverse safety requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
