<p><p>The vacuum connectors market is categorized into three main types based on pressure levels: High Vacuum, Ultra High Vacuum (UHV), and Extreme High Vacuum (XHV). High Vacuum connectors operate in the range of 10^-3 to 10^-9 torr, suitable for various industrial applications. UHV connectors function within the 10^-9 to 10^-12 torr range, ideal for research and semiconductor manufacturing. XHV connectors operate below 10^-12 torr, used in advanced scientific research and particle physics, requiring superior sealing and material properties.</p></p>
