<p><p>The compostable packaging materials market is primarily divided into two categories: compostable plastics and compostable paper. Compostable plastics are made from renewable resources like cornstarch or sugarcane, designed to break down in industrial composting facilities, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional plastics. Compostable paper, on the other hand, is derived from wood pulp and treated to decompose efficiently, making it suitable for food packaging and other applications. Both types significantly reduce environmental impact by minimizing waste and enhancing biodegradability.</p></p>
