<p><p>The Door Handle Harness market is divided into two main types: In-Door Wiring Harnesses and Under The Door Harnesses. In-Door Wiring Harnesses are integrated within the vehicle's door, connecting various electrical components to ensure seamless operation of features such as windows and locks. Both types enhance vehicle safety, convenience, and functionality while contributing to overall automotive design efficiency.</p></p>
