<p><ul><li>U Type</li><li>Pin Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive automatic transmission shift lock mechanism market includes two primary types: U Type and Pin Type. The U Type mechanism employs a curved design to securely lock the gear shift lever, preventing accidental movement out of the park position. In contrast, the Pin Type utilizes a pin that engages with the transmission system, ensuring the shift lever remains locked until the brake pedal is activated. Both types enhance vehicle safety by preventing unintended gear shifts, catering to the growing demand for reliable automotive safety features.</p></p>
