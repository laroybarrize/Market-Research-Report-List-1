<p><p>The steam safety valve market is categorized into low-pressure steam valves and high-pressure steam valves, each designed for specific applications. Low-pressure steam valves are typically used in systems operating below 15 psi, ensuring safe operation in processes like heating and food production. Conversely, high-pressure steam valves cater to systems exceeding 15 psi, commonly found in industrial applications such as power generation and petrochemical processing. Both types play a crucial role in preventing overpressure and ensuring equipment safety across diverse industries.</p></p>
