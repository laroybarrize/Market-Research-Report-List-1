<p><p>The polyester grafts market is comprised of two primary types: knitted and woven polyester vascular grafts. Knitted polyester vascular grafts are made using a interlocking stitch technique, offering flexibility and a smooth inner surface, ideal for various vascular applications. Woven polyester vascular grafts, on the other hand, utilize a weaving technique that provides excellent strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure environments. Both types are crucial in medical procedures for treating vascular diseases and ensuring proper blood flow.</p></p>
