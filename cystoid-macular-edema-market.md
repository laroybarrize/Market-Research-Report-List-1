Anti-Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor (VEGF) agents target abnormal blood vessel growth, crucial in managing CME. Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors decrease fluid accumulation in the retina, promoting better vision. Steroids, characterized by their strong anti-inflammatory properties, are often utilized to reduce swelling and inflammation associated with CME. Each type plays a distinct role in the overall management of this condition.</p></p>
