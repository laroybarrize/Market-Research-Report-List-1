<p><ul><li>Primary Knee Replacement</li><li>Partial Knee Replacement</li><li>Revision Knee Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knee replacement devices market consists of three main types: primary knee replacement, partial knee replacement, and revision knee replacement. Primary knee replacement involves replacing the entire knee joint with artificial components, typically for severe arthritis. Partial knee replacement replaces only the damaged compartment of the knee, preserving healthy tissue for patients with localized issues. Revision knee replacement addresses complications or failures from previous surgeries, requiring the removal and replacement of existing implants to restore function and alleviate pain.</p></p>
