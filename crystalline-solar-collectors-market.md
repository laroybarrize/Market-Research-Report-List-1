<p><p>Crystalline solar collectors primarily include Flat Plate Collectors and Evacuated Tube Collectors. Flat Plate Collectors consist of a collectors' surface absorbing sunlight, suitable for moderate temperature applications. In contrast, Evacuated Tube Collectors feature multiple tubes, creating a vacuum that enhances efficiency in colder climates. Additionally, the market encompasses other types such as Concentrated Solar Power systems, which use mirrors to focus sunlight for higher efficiency. Together, these technologies cater to various energy needs in residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
