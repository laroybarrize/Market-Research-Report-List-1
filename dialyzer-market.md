<p><p>Dialyzer market types include Hollow Fiber, Coil Tube, and Flat Type dialyzers, each designed for specific renal therapies. Hollow Fiber dialyzers use thousands of tiny fibers for efficient filtration, making them common in hemodialysis. Coil Tube dialyzers utilize a coiled design, enhancing surface area for improved blood purification, often used in continuous renal replacement therapy. Flat Type dialyzers feature flat membranes, offering simplicity and versatility in various applications. Each type serves to optimize patient care in kidney treatment.</p></p>
