<p><ul><li>Thread Ring Gages</li><li>Threaded Plug Gages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thread gages market encompasses two primary types: thread ring gages and threaded plug gages. Thread ring gages are used to measure the external dimensions of threaded parts, ensuring they meet specified tolerances. In contrast, threaded plug gages are designed for internal threading, verifying that holes or sockets are accurately sized. Both types are crucial in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, where precision in threaded components is essential for functionality and safety.</p></p>
