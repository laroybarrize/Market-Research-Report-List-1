<p><ul><li>Hartmann</li><li>Rydel-Seiffer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical tuning fork market is primarily categorized into two types: Hartmann and Rydel-Seiffer forks. Hartmann tuning forks are commonly used for general auditory tests, contributing to quick assessments of hearing loss. Rydel-Seiffer tuning forks, on the other hand, offer dual-frequency capabilities, enabling more precise evaluations of both auditory and vibratory sensations. Each type serves specific diagnostic purposes in clinical settings, catering to the varied needs of healthcare professionals in assessing auditory health and neurological functions.</p></p>
