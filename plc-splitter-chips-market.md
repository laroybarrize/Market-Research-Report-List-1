<p><p>PLC splitter chips are integral to fiber optic networks, enabling signal distribution across multiple fibers. The market is categorized into two main types: 1 X N PLC splitter chips, which split a single input signal into N output signals, optimizing space and reducing costs for various applications. In contrast, 2 X N PLC splitter chips manage two input signals, enhancing flexibility in managing dual-channel signals while maintaining efficient power distribution. Both types are crucial for expanding fiber optic connectivity in telecommunications.</p></p>
